4 Parabolic

Lemma 15 Parabolic lemma

parabolic Let \(R\) be a reversible occurrence net, and \(m \llbracket {\omega } \rangle _{\{ \! \! \{ R\} \! \! \} } m'\) a firing sequence. Then there exist two sequences of transitions, \(\omega '\) and \(\omega ''\), such that \(\omega '\) consists only of backward transitions, \(\omega ''\) consists only of forward transitions, \(\omega \asymp _{\{ \! \! \{ R \} \! \! \} } \omega '; \omega ''\), and \(m \llbracket {\omega ’; \omega ”}\rangle _{\{ \! \! \{ R \} \! \! \} } m'\).
